# Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week, subject to manager approval and role suitability.

Fully remote arrangements require written approval from both the reporting manager and HR, and are reviewed every 6 months.

Employees working remotely must be available during core hours, 10 AM to 4 PM IST, and must attend all scheduled team meetings via video call.

The company does not provide equipment stipends for remote work setups at this time.
