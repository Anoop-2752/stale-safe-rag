# Expense Reimbursement Policy

All business-related expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date, along with a valid receipt.

Travel expenses including flights, hotels, and local transport are reimbursed at actual cost, provided they fall within the approved travel budget set by the manager.

Meal expenses during business travel are capped at 1500 INR per day. Alcohol is not reimbursable under any circumstances.

Reimbursements are processed within 10 working days of approval and are credited directly to the employee's registered bank account.
